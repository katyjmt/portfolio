About

Hi, I'm Katy! I've been working in the tech industry for about 7 years now. Over this time I've worn multiple hats; the projects I've worked on have required me to be a project manager, front-end developer, UX consultant, designer, strategist, data analyst and account manager. I love creating beautiful and useful things, and get deep satisfaction out of working out how to do something from first principles. Over the years I've done deep-dives into front-end coding, SQL, typeface design, book binding, sewing and patternmaking, how to fit-out a camper van, and lots of other creative and analytical pursuits. Currently I'm completing a full-stack coding bootcamp.

Over my professional life, I've collaborated closely with talented teams of developers and designers on website and microsite projects for renowned brands like Sydney Symphony Orchestra, Bega, Mars, Fitness First, Warner Music, and more. The projects I've worked on, and the people I've worked with have taught me a lot about what good design is. How to create something that's not just aesthetically delightful, but that is designed around a deep understanding of the user and their needs.

This website is a place for me to document my personal projects and explorations. I'll be adding to it more and more as I work through my Coding bootcamp.

Evie Sans Font

Glyphs App

Evie Sans was developed in 2020 as a typeface to be used for my customisable diary company PLANNER.STUDIO. The fonts have geometric proportions and subtle Art Deco influences in several of the uppercase characters, including a lowered crossbar on the H, lowered middle arm on the F and deepened bowl on the P.

Bake & Groove

HTML / CSS / JS / Tailwind / JQuery

Bake & Groove is a recipe generator application for anyone that is unsure of what to cook, and needs a little bit of inspiration to get started. The user can select a type of cuisine and choose from one of many recipe options. Their choice will be accompanied by a groovin' playlist to match their taste buds.
